My Favourite Meal

Fish Soup

A Delightful meal made with Fish

Fish soup is quite a dish like we have rarely seen before. It is a noodle soup. This soup is considered the a congolese dish and is eaten for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Some eats for breakfast dish (and what a delightful breakfast it would make!) while some enjoys at any part of the day. If you’re craving for some fish soup in the middle of the afternoon in downtown Yangon, chances are good you’ll quickly find a street vendor to serve you a fresh batch.

  • Steps/Method to Prepare Fish Soup
    • Wash, clean the fish and remove the bones from it. Then, slice the fish into very small pieces.
    • Take a bowl, add the fish pieces along with salt, black pepper, ajinomoto (reserving a little for further use), eggs and arrowroot powder in it. Mix well.
    • Make small balls from the mixture
    • Take a pan, keep it on low flame and add water along with fish balls to it.
